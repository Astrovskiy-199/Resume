Software testing course for beginners

The idea of conducting the courses appeared quite accidentally, in the kitchen of Pixelplus company) I asked our HR, Daria Zhuravskaya, if the idea of creating SOFTWARE testing courses for the company was interesting. After all, during the course, everyone wins, both students, the teacher and the company. The advantage of a teacher is that he gets valuable experience mentoring Junior employees and repeats already forgotten theory in places. For students, the advantage is the opportunity to get a first job and get basic knowledge in the field of testing, and the company is guaranteed to get the most motivated and interested Junior employees who have already met the company during the course. To my surprise, the next day a meeting was organized about the course, where the goals and objectives were discussed, and then it was decided to organize recruitment for the course. As a result of completing the course, two Junior testers received job offers.

One of the main tasks during the courses was to minimize stressful situations and competition between students, and also to create maximum mutual assistance and teamwork. Aetsoft

Aetsoft specializes in the development and development of international fintech projects on the blockchain, mobile games, web projects using highly loaded systems. specializes in the following areas: Blockchain, AI & Machine Learning, Computer Vision, IoT, AR / VR / Mixed Reality.

I worked for the company for a year and a half. A lot of good experiences. Initially, I was a content Manager at this company and did minimal site layout, but then a fairly serious project appeared that required QA. I was offered to try this profession, as a result of which I agreed. We agreed that I would spend two months learning the basics of testing and development, and then the development team would conduct an interview, the results of which would suggest that I stay or leave the company. During the two months of preparation, I read the main books on testing, studied the most relevant tools, and successfully passed the interview. That's when my QA journey started. The company had mainly blockchain projects and testing was based on them. For the most part, I tested API services and frontend, but sometimes I had to fix some minor bugs myself.

PixelPlex

One of the world's leading blockchain development companies. The portfolio includes more than 50 implemented projects, including not only blockchain solutions, but also mobile games, virtual reality applications, online stores, etc

After I joined PixelPlex, I really found out what the IT movement is: a large number of projects where everyone has their own methodology, daily rallies, sprints, huge amounts of documentation, lack of time and corrections from customers. There were periods that in one week it was necessary to test 7 or more projects, it was quite difficult, but very interesting, in this mode I learned how to estimate the time for tests most accurately and to approach testing optimally. In such stressful times, experience increased many times over. The projects were diverse, from mobile games to online stores and blockchain solutions
