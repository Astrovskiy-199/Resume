Hi, nice to meet you!

There are people for whom searching for bugs is a daily job. These are completely ordinary people who live among us. We often meet them in the store, in the cinema, on the street, and do not even suspect that each of them has a huge number of saved projects, fixed bugs, and even improvements. They are constantly putting themselves at risk for the project, they don't know fear, they don't know doubt, they don't know what cowardice is. These are people with a strong character, iron willpower, and a strong spirit. People in this profession have a duty to look for bugs, while making every possible and impossible effort. These heroes are testers.
